Concert Review

Stray Kids dominATE World Tour in Singapore

I got the VIP ticket last minute and managed to get it with a cheaper price. I also made new friends there. My seat number was Row 10-28. I got goosebumps when I saw the artists for the first time during soundcheck. I enjoyed the whole concert, but my neck hurts so much because people in front of me are too tall. So, I couldn't enjoy the concert properly and they keep raising their hands high. I'm glad enough to be able to watch them in real life for the first time.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Stray Kids dominATE World Tour in Jakarta

I got the VIP ticket by finding it earlier since I'm picky about where I want to sit. I do not want to sit in the front row but would like to sit at the back but near the stage, so I could see them properly. During the concert, I was able to get closer to the stage. This concert made up for the one in Singapore since I couldn't enjoy it last time, but I am very much satisfied since Indonesia fans are tolerable.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Aespa Synk Parallel Line World Tour in Bangkok

I got the normal ticket category for this concert since I just wanted to get the vibes of the concert. I enjoyed the setlist so far and I would love to go again to the concert next time. My seat was very comfortable, and I could still see the artists from afar.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
