Stray Kids

About Group

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a 8-member South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment, consisting of Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. They debuted on March 25, 2018, with their first mini album, I am NOT. The group was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. As of February 10, 2022, they are also signed under Republic Records.

Official Greeting: “Step Out! Hello, we are Stray Kids!”

Stray Kids Official Fandom Name: STAY
