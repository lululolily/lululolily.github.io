About Group

ATEEZ (ÏóêÏù¥Ìã∞Ï¶à), known pre-debut as KQ Fellaz, is a 8-member boy group under KQ Entertainment. The group consists of: Seonghwa, Hongjoong, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho. They released the pre-debut song, From on July 9, 2018 with another trainee named Junyoung. The group debuted on October 24, 2018 with their first mini album, Treasure Ep.1: All to Zero.
